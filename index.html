A community of Latin American cryptographers

Hi! Welcome to our site!

Criptolatino is a global community of Latin American cryptographers. We bring together researchers from around the world to foster connections and networking among our members. Our mission is to make their research visible, highlight their contributions, and support their careers. This site also shares opportunities for this underrepresented group in the global South.

We hope this site helps you connect with our members and learn about their research activities.
